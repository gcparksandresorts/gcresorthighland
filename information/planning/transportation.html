Public Transport

Oras Nou features many useful and budget-friendly public transit options for guests visiting the resort without a vehicle.

Public Bus Routes

Estimated Cost: $2.50

Depending on where you are in Oras Nou, there are multiple ways to get to the Globe-City Resort Highland by standard bus route. The main bus terminal near the resort is the Convention Center Terminal. If entering here, head north towards Adventure Prkwy to enter City-Shop. Then follow signs.
  • Station: #80012 - Convention Center Terminal
  • Standard Routes: 108, 63, 40, 77, 188
  • Rapid Routes: OCEAN 4, 208, 288

Public Trams

Estimated Cost: $2.50

There are two tram routes servicing the station closest to the resort. Upon entering, cross the bridge from the station to Adventure Parkway and head north.

From Oras Nou International Airport

  • Station: #T807
  • Suggested Route: BLUE LINE S

From Union Station (Downtown Oras Nou)

  • Station: #T807
  • Suggested Route: BLUE LINE N

From Highland / Oceanside Transit Center

  • Station: #T807
  • Suggested Route: PURPLE LOOP
For those visiting with a personal or rented vehicle, parking is available for both hotels, adventure park, and city shop
