Public Transport

🛪 Oras Nou International Aiport (O-Loop, Airport, Interstate Rail)

Follow signs for the Airport City Transit Center, then take one of the following train or bus lines:

  • BLUE LINE S (Trolley) - to Convention Center Terminal
  • 708 (Union Station) (Rapid Bus) - to Convention Center Terminal

Then follow signs for Globe-City Resort Highland

Estimated Cost: $2.50 one-way

Alternate Transit: Use the complimentary GC RESORT HIGHLAND shuttle service. For guests with Hotel or Themepark reservations only.

🚄 Downtown Union Station (O-Loop, Interstate Busses, Trolley)

Follow signs for the Oras Nou Trolley BLUE Line or 708 / 180 busses

  • 708 (Airport City) (Rapid Bus) - to Convention Center Terminal
  • 180 (Oceanside) (Bus) - to Convention Center Terminal
  • BLUE LINE N (Trolley) - to Convention Center Terminal

Then follow signs for Globe-City Resort Highland

Estimated Cost: $2.50 one-way

🦒 Oras Nou Zoo (Newtown)

Navigate to the Oras Nou Zoo Transit Center

  1. Step 1: YELLOW LINE E (Trolley) - to Downtown Union Station
  2. Step 2: BLUE LINE N (Trolley) OR 708 (Airport City) / 180 (Oceanside) (Bus) - to Convention Center Terminal

Then follow signs for Globe-City Resort Highland

Estimated cost: $5.00 one-way

🚢 Port of Oras Nou Terminal (Ferries, Cruiselines, Interstate Rail and Bus)

Alternate Route (Via Oceanside Transit Center)

Shuttle Services

Globe-City Resort Shuttle Service

Includes non-stop service from the Oras Nou International Airport to the Globe-City Resort Highland.

Shuttles board at the Airport City Transit Center. Follow signs for DOCK SIX. Look for navy blue busses with the Globe-City logo.

There are two Globe-City Resort shuttles, one for HIGHLAND and the other for OCEANSIDE. Make sure to board the HIGHLAND shuttle only.

Cost: Shuttle Service is free for guests with Lakeside Hotel, Aztech Resort, or Adventure Park reservations / tickets. All other guests will be charged a $20 / per person fee.

Convention Center Shuttle

The Convention Center Shuttle is a paid service which transports guests to and from the Convention Center Transit Terminal. It has stops at both Union Station in Downtown and Airport City Transit Center outside the Oras Nou International Airport.

Cost: $15 per person
