Globe-City Resort Highland Transportation Information

Public Transport

Select a starting point to view transit directions

🛪 Oras Nou International Aiport (O-Loop, Airport, Interstate Rail)

Follow signs for the Airport City Transit Center, then take one of the following train or bus lines:

  • BLUE LINE S (Trolley) - to Convention Center Terminal
  • 708 (Union Station) (Rapid Bus) - to Convention Center Terminal

Then follow signs for Globe-City Resort Highland

Estimated Cost: $2.50 one-way

🚄 Downtown Union Station (O-Loop, Interstate Busses, Trolley)

Follow signs for the Oras Nou Trolley BLUE Line or 708 / 180 busses

  • 708 (Airport City) (Rapid Bus) - to Convention Center Terminal
  • 180 (Oceanside) (Bus) - to Convention Center Terminal
  • BLUE LINE N (Trolley) - to Convention Center Terminal

Then follow signs for Globe-City Resort Highland

Estimated Cost: $2.50 one-way

🦒 Oras Nou Zoo (Newtown)

Navigate to the Oras Nou Zoo Transit Center

  1. Step 1: YELLOW LINE E (Trolley) - to Downtown Union Station
  2. Step 2: BLUE LINE N (Trolley) OR 708 (Airport City) / 180 (Oceanside) (Bus) - to Convention Center Terminal

Then follow signs for Globe-City Resort Highland

Estimated cost: $5.00 one-way

🚢 Port of Oras Nou Terminal (Ferries, Cruiselines, Interstate Rail and Bus)

  • BLUE LINE N (Trolley) - to Convention Center Terminal

Estimated cost: $2.50

Alternate Route (Via Oceanside Transit Center)

  1. Step 1: SEA LINE N (Trolley) - to Oceanside Transit Center
  2. Step 2: PURPLE LOOP (Clockwise) (Trolley) - to Convention Center Terminal

Estimated cost: $2.50
