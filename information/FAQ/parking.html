Resort Parking

Parking at the Globe-City Resort Highland

There are multiple parking locations within the resort including at our two resort hotels and City-Shop.

City-Shop parking lots are for City-Shop guests only. Resort Hotel parking is for Hotel guests only.

Adventure Parking Lot

For guests and annual passholders visiting Adventure Park, please use the Adventure Parking lot.

Parking Rates

  • Tier 1-2 Days: $18
  • Tier 3-4 Days: $24
  • Tier 5 Days: $30
To access the Adventure Parking lot, please enter through the North-Gate off of Adventure Parkway. For navigation, the address of the Adventure Parking lot entrance is 1003 Adventure Prkwy. Highland, ON 92100

Overflow Parking

On busier days or for special events, guests may be directed to the Cosmic Parking Lot for overflow. This parking lot is accessible at 1015 Adventure Parkwy. Highland, ON 92100. Adventure Parking rates apply to the Cosmic Parking lot as well.
