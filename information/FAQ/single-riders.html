Single Riders Program

Save Time Waiting in Line

With the Single Riders program, guests willing to ride alone and wishing to shorten their wait can utilize the Single Riders queues. Single Riders are used to fill empty seats on attractions to increase capacity. Single Riders will be unable to ride with their group

Guests must be 12 years or older to ride by themselves.

Important Single Riders Guidelines

Single Riders queues may close periodically depending on capacity and queue times.

Single riders cannot ride together, you will be split apart from your group to fill empty seats.

Single riders cannot make seating requests (I.E. requesting the first row)

At this time, guests with wheelchairs or requiring assistance with boarding cannot use the single riders queues.

Guests caught jumping from single riders queues into standby or GC VIP queues will be kicked from Adventure Park

Guests wishing to ride together will be directed to the entrance of the standby queue, even if they already waited in the single riders queue.

How to Utilize Single Riders

The following attractions currently feature Single Riders queues:
Mission 12, Ride the Beat: Waveriders, Touch the Sky, Tomb of the Emperor's Curse, Old Yander's Mine Train, Crusader

Follow signs or ask attraction crew members for the location of the Single Riders queue entrance.
