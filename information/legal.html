Annual Passports Terms and Conditions

Last Updated August 21, 2023

The following terms and conditions ("Terms") apply to the 12-Month Annual Passports ("Passports") available for Adventure Park, located within Globe-City Resort Highland. By purchasing and using any of the Passports, you agree to adhere to these Terms. Please read them carefully before purchasing a Passport.

1. Passports Overview

(a) We offer three types of Passports: Resident, Adventure, and Platinum. Each Passport comes with specific benefits, as outlined below.

  • Resident Passport: Designed for residents of Oras Nou, Minecraftia. This passport grants access to Adventure Park during regular operating hours on dates listed as Tier 1, Tier 2, or Tier 3. Proof of residency is required for purchase.
  • Adventure Passport: This passport provides access to Adventure Park during operating hours on dates listed as Tier 4 or lower. The Adventure passport can be purchased by any guest including those outside of Oras Nou and Minecraftia. The Adventure Passport includes free parking in the Adventure Parking lot only, and discounts on merchandise and dining inside Adventure Park only (restrictions apply for third-party vendors)
  • Platinum Passport: The Platinum Passport offers unlimited access to Adventure Park, along with exclusive benefits such as priority queue entry, free parking, discounts on merchandise and dining, and access to VIP Lounges where applicable. Exact restrictions for priority queue access and other benefits are available on the Annual Passport information page

2. Passport Purchase and Usage

(a) Passports are available for purchase at designated locations within Adventure Park, at the Esplenade Ticket Booths, and online through our website.

(b) Passport holders must present a valid Adventure Park Passport and a photo ID for initial admission to Adventure Park. On following visits passport holders will need to provide the passport as well as a biometric reading for admission to the park.

(c) Passports are non-transferable, non-refundable, and cannot be exchanged for cash.

3. Validity

(a) Each Passport is valid for a period of 12 months from the date of purchase.

(b) The Passport is only valid during regular operating hours of Adventure Park and may not be used during special events or private functions unless additional privleges have been awarded.

(c) Globe-City Parks and Resorts reserves the right to adjust operating hours, close the park for maintenance or other reasons, or deny entry due to capacity constraints.

4. Benefits and Privileges

(a) The benefits and privileges associated with each Passport type are subject to change without notice.

(b) Platinum Passport holders may receive additional benefits, including priority access, discounts, and invitations to special events. Privileges are subject to change or temporary freeze. These benefits are outlined in detail on our website. Special privileges may be revoked at any time for any reason.

5. Passport Renewal

(a) Passport holders may renew their Passports within 14 days prior to the expiration date to receive a discounted renewal rate.

(b) Failure to renew a Passport within 30 days after the expiration date will require the purchase of a new Passport at the regular price.

6. Lost or Stolen Passports

(a) In case of a lost or stolen Passport, please notify the Passholder Service Desk - (312) 988 1300 - immediately.

(b) A replacement fee will apply for lost or stolen Passports. Proof of purchase and identification will be required for replacement.

7. Park Rules and Regulations

(a) Passport holders are subject to Adventure Park's rules and regulations. Violation of these rules may result in the revocation of Passport privileges without refund.

(b) Adventure Park reserves the right to refuse entry or remove any individual from the park for reasons including but not limited to inappropriate behavior, failure to follow park rules, and failure to comply with park staff instructions
