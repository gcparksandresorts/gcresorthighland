Boardwalk Bistro

Dining located in Boardwalk in Adventure Park

Take your taste buds to the Bay Area with artisan pastas, sandwiches made with fresh-baked breads, and salads inspired by the northern regions of the Minecraftian Coast. Order at the counter, then bring your food out into the sun and dine on the lively boardwalk as coasters zoom by.
