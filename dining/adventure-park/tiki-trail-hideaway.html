Tiki Trail Hideaway

Dining located in Adventure Plains in Adventure Park

Your Passport to Paradise! Embark on a culinary adventure deep into the heart of the jungle at Tiki Trail Hideaway! Experience the thrill of exploration as you indulge in tantalizing flavors inspired by the exotic wonders of Polynesia and the Jungle as you unwind in our tropical oasis. Escape the ordinary and immerse yourself in the enchanting ambiance of our rustic yet vibrant hideaway.
