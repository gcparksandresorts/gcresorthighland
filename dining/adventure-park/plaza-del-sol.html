Plaza Del Sol

Dining located in Adventure Plains in Adventure Park

Plaza Del Sol, meaning Plaza of the Sun, is a place where anyone can come to discover Mexican and Spanish cuisine, culture, and art. Featuring an authentic restaurant serving up spanish and mexico inspired dishes, a plaza stage home to talented Mariachi performers, and a quaint Tienda selling classic spanish goods, there's much to explore! Dine under mexican flag inspired flags strung from colorful lights and enjoy live performances plus a splendid view of the swirling Fiesta Del Sol ride.
