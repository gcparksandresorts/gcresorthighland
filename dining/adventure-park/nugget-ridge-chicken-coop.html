Nugget Ridge Chicken Coop

Dining located in Old Yander's Boom Town in Adventure Park

At Nugget Ridge, we're all about hearty, satisfying fare that fuels your spirit for adventure. Indulge in our hand-breaded fried chicken sandwiches, each bite a golden treasure of crispy goodness. Savor the flavors of the frontier with our tantalizing salads, wings, and sides, crafted with the freshest ingredients and a dash of pioneer spirit. Relax in our rustic yet welcoming atmosphere, where the spirit of the gold rush era comes to life around you. Whether you're fueling up for your next expedition or taking a well-deserved break from the thrills of the park, Nugget Ridge Chicken Coop is your oasis of comfort and flavor.
