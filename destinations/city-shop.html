Minecraftia's Most Advanced Mall

Developed by Globe-City Creative Developments, City-Shop boasts an immersive audio-visual experience like none other, featuring a cutting-edge sound system with over 300 speakers and subwoofers distributed across the 0.8 mile shopping center.

As the sun sets, enjoy synchronized music and dazzling visuals from over 15,000 LEDs, projectors, and other technologies in wonderful art. At the North end of City-Shop, find over two dozen dancing water fountains synchronized to the music, complemented by a massive 500,000 pixel screen.
