Soak in the Sun by the Pool

Globe-City's Lakeside Hotel features a large pool deck with a heated pool and waterslide, two spas, cabanas, and a full-service bar and snack stand.

In addition to splashing fun, the hotel also features an indoor 24hr fitness center, laundry, a grab n' go convinience store, and an arts + crafts room for families.
