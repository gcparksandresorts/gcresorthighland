Ride the Beat: Waveriders to recieve a totally awesome major update

Painter Creative

April 1, 2024

We promise it's no April Fools joke, but Ride the Beat: Waveriders is soon to recieve a brand new refresh as part of 2024's SummerFest Celebration!

This is the first major update to the attraction since its update in 2021 in which the ride gained its "Waveriders" subtitle. The new update will include:

  • FOUR Brand New Audio Tracks - your ride audio will depend on the vehicle you ride in!
  • New Projections in certain parts of the ride
  • Updated queue audio and decorations
  • A re-recorded and updated pre-show video
  • General appearence updates
  • Minor operational improvements

As one of our most popular attractions at the Globe-City Resort Highland, recieving over 4 million riders annually, we want to ensure that every rider has a more-than-awesome experience. At Globe-City Parks and Resorts, we are always improving!
