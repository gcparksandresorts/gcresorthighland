Nytz Tavern Presents Saturday Nightz at City-Shop Highland!

MRU Marketing

March 2024

Every Saturday Night in March, live it up with a free-access DJ Dance Party at Fountain Plaza in City-Shop!

Welcome to a brand new era of entertainment at City-Shop in Highland, Oras Nou!

This March, live your Highland Night adventure with free-to-access dance parties at Globe-City's City-Shop Fountain Plaza. Featuring different DJs every saturday and a unique theme for all four nights, its the ultimate social Saturday event!

Event Details

  • Hours - 9:00 PM to 12:00 AM
  • Location - Fountain Plaza, City-Shop
  • Entry - Free of charge! Wristband Required

Each of the 4 Saturday Nightz events will feature a unique theme and matching setlist, spun by some of Oras Nou's best up-and-coming DJs. In addition to the entertainment, pop-up drink stations provided by Nyghtz Tavern will feature themed beverages to further enhance your night.

Nightz Themes

  • Night 1: Western

    Bring your best western get-up for some swinging fun featuring cowboys and some gold-ridden drinks

  • Night 2: Sci-Fi

    Travel through the cosmos with alien drinks and an even spacier setlist

  • Night 3: The Golden Age

    Dress up as some of your favorite movie characters from the Golden Age of cinema!

  • Night 4: Sports

    Adorn yourself in a Jersey or a baseball cap and come dance the night away in honors of sports
