Preview: The Highland Adventure Project

Globe-City Creative Developments

September 25

At this year's Industry Conference, the Globe-City Entertainment panel officially announced plans for The Highland Adventure Project, a multi-stage million dollar project to expand the Globe-City Resort Highland over its existing land holdings.

In this article, we will detail the expansion plans and how this is going to effect the operations of the Globe-City Resort Highland. We're very excited to finally be talking about this project!

Once the project officially begins, we will be releasing the official Adventure Project website which will provide an interactive portal for guests, investors, and city planners to explore the project in full and learn about the exciting new prospects coming to Highland.

About the Announcement

The Highland Adventure Project was announced at the annual Industry Conference put on by R and R Industries on September 25th at the Oras Nou Expo Center in Downtown. In the audience were R and R employees, investors, stake holders, as well as public leaders and administration, and more.

The announcement was made by the Co-Chair of Globe-City Entertainment LLC. and CEO of Parks and Resorts, Reuven Reyman, as well as GCPR's magic-making team at Globe-City Creative Developments.

The Adventure Project announcement made at IC is a preview to the project which has yet to be put into action. The official "release date" of the Adventure Project and all information regarding will be January 1st, 2024 and will be marked with the release of the Adventure Project website and opening of the Adventure Project preview center in City-Shop.

Introduction to the Highland Adventure Project

The Highland Adventure Project is a multi-stage expansion and improvement project that will take place over 10 years. The project sees to improve the guest experience at the Globe-City Resort Highland and expand our offerings, attractions, and guest-accessible space. The project will take place in chronological stages with each stage being completed in full before the next stage begins.

Basic Preliminary Timeline

  • 2024 - Project is finalized and announced, startup of the website
  • Late 2024 - Stage 1 beggins: 'Parking Forward North'
  • 2025 - Stage 1 is completed. Stage 2 begins: 'Second Gate'
  • 2026 - Globe-City Resort Highland 10th Anniversary Celebration!
  • Late 2026 - Stage 2 is nearly done, preparation and training begin.
  • 2027 - Stage 2 ends. Brief pause on project.
  • 2028 - Investor review. Project check-in
  • 2029 - Stage 3 begins: 'Animation Project'
  • 2030 - End of stage 3. Late 2030 - Beggining of Stage 4: 'Speed'
  • 2032 - End of stage 4. Grace period.
  • 2033 - Beggining of stage 5: 'Parking Forward South'
  • 2034 - Official Completion of The Adventure Project
Important Note: This timeline is extremley prototyped and will change as the Adventure Project comes closer to being finalized.

Highland Community Impact

From the beggining, the Globe-City Resort Highland has put the wants, needs, and requests of our surrounding Highland District community first. We are greatful to be a part of the bustling entertainment capitol and have greatly enjoyed improving the Highland community landscape.

At its completion, The Adventure Project plans to bring upwards of 200 Million more visitors to the Highland resort area. This influx of tourism will increase foot traffic around Highland, driving the revenue of local community buisnesses. Unlike Disney and other corporate entities, Globe-City Parks and Resorts does not plan to drive guests away from local buisnesses, and will always work with local community partners to ensure Highland is included in our projects.
