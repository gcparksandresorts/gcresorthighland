Discover the Amazing Technology Fueling City-Shop at the Globe-City Resort Highland

Reuven Reyman

February 10, 2024

Pioneered by Globe-City Creative Developments, City-Shop is one of the most advanced audio-visual displays in Minecraftia. Hear music like never before with a massive sound system powered by Aztech Audio and LAB Sound Technology featuring over 300 speakers and subwoofers hidden throughout the 0.8 mile shopping center totalling over 300,000 Watts.

When the sun sets on City-Shop, you won't just hear the music, you'll see it too. Every musical track is hand-programmed to play in sync with over 15000 LEDs spread throughout the entire shopping-center in beautiful color-changing delight. Accompanying the LEDs are several projectors, laser scanners, and moving heads to create an eye-popping visual experience that can only be considered Art.

Walk down to the North end of City-Shop where you'll find over 2 dozen dancing water fountains synced to the music and backdropped by a massive 500,000 pixel screen.
