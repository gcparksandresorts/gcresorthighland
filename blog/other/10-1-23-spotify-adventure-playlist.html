Spotify Brings the Adventure to Your Phone

Gregory Hannah

October 1

Ladies and Gentlemen, boys and girls, the official Globe-City Adventure Park music pack is here! Thanks to Spotify, now you can listen to Adventure Park and feel right in the heart of your favorite themepark - wherever you are!

The collection is comprised of multiple playlists, each one containing the sounds of an Adventure Park land, ride, or other music area. From lively swing of The Boulevard and classic 80's sounds of The Boulevard Diner to the delightful music of Boardwalk and relaxing sounds of the Tiki Bar in Adventure Plains. All of your favorite sounds are now in one place! And the best part: they are free to listen to, wherever you may be!

To listen, simply download Spotify and create an account. Then search Globe-City Adventure Park and discover the music of the park! Or, you can find the embeded playlists below to start listening right away!
