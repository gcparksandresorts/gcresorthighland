The Painter Family Tree Lighting Ceramony

Reuven Reyman

November 10th

It's the most magical time of year! Snow has once again began to fall along The Boulevard at Globe-City's Adventure Park as we welcome in the Holiday Season! The beggining of this wonderful season also signals the annual Tree Lighting Ceramony as the humongous Entrance Plaza Christmas Tree ignites the night!

This year, the Globe-City Resort would like to honor some very important people, Jacob Painter and Will, credited as two of the most influential people in bringing the Adventure Park idea to life! As a special thanks, the Globe-City Resort has dedicated this year's tree lighting to Jacob and their family for playing an integral role in shaping the renowned resort you experience today.

Event Details

The Tree Lighting Ceramony will begin at 7:00PM on the very first night of Globe-City's Season of Light Celebration, November 17th.

A shorter version of the ceramony will also take place on Christmas Eve, the night of December 24th.

The ceramony will consist of live holiday music, demonstrations by the Oras Nou Unified School District's choir, a dedication speech by CEO of Parks and Resorts, Reuven Reyman, and finally a grand lighting ceramony with music and fireworks.

Special admission is not required for the ceramony and is included with regular admission and applicable annual passports.

Viewing for the event includes standing viewing around the Entrance Plaza and along The Boulevard. Fireworks will be launched from atop the themepark main entry booths as well as the rooftops of The Boulevard Facades.

We hope you can join us for the Painter Family Tree Lighting Ceramony, and for an amazing Holiday Season. We can't wait for this year's Season of Light Celebration and all the memories to be made here at the Globe-City Resort Highland!

See you at the snow!
