Dia de Los Muertos at Adventure Park

Marketing Team

September 10

Join Us for a Spectacular Dia de los Muertos Celebration at Plaza del México! Starting September 1st all the way to November 2.

Event Highlights

Live Entertainment

There's something to discover every hour! With live Mariachi Music, beautifully decorated dancers, and Day-of-the-Dead story telling. Witness captivating folkloric dance performances that showcase the beauty and energy of Mexican culture. Check the Resort Information page for showtimes.

Stunning Decorations

Plaza Del Mexico is almost unrecognizable with a brand new burst of color and cultural decorations in every square inch. Explore intricately decorated ofrendas (altars) adorned with marigolds, candles, and sugar skulls that pay tribute to loved ones who have passed away. You may even bring your own family member's photo to add.

Face Painting

Get your face painted with stunning Dia de los Muertos designs to truly embrace the spirit of the celebration.

Delicious Cuisine

Savor the flavors of Mexico with an array of mouthwatering Mexican dishes, including tacos, churros, and more!

Important Information

Admission

This event is included with your theme park admission. No additional tickets needed!
Don't miss this opportunity to celebrate Dia de los Muertos in all its glory, right here at Plaza del México. It's a day of remembrance, love, and festivity that's perfect for the whole family! We look forward to celebrating with you!
