September 9

It's officially the beggining of one of our favorite seasons here at the Globe-City Resort Highland! Leaves are beggining to turn brown and purple and green lighting has turned up around Adventure Park. Halloween has arrived -- well the Halloween Season that is ;)

Halloween Season Dates and Important Events

  • September 1st - Decorations arrive
  • September 9th - The Halloween Season officially begins!
  • September 20th - First night of Sally's Spooktacular Halloween Party!
  • October 31st - The end of the Halloween season and our Spooktacular Closing Ceramony

Thing's to Look Forward To

Stunning Decorations

Watch as the resort is reborn into dazzling lights, garland, and - of course - pumpkins! Walk down Adventure Boulevard draped in ornate decorations and stroll through Hindsborg village with eery lighting and creepy new atmospheric audio. The whole park is in the Halloween spirit!

Tasty New Treats

Visit our in-park resturaunts and snack carts for new Spooky Treats sure to cause delight! Our Creative Culinary team has been working around the clock to curate new treats that are sure to bring a smile to even the most ghostly of faces

New Characters to Meet

During Sally's Halloween After-Party, meet new frightfully fun characters roaming throughout the park! From some familiar faces dressed up in their best Halloween attire to some new ones ready to cause a stir. During the day, meet some of your favorites in their custom Halloween outfits and costumes as they prepare for the party!

New Merchandise for Homey Frights

Make sure to checkout our in-park retail stores for some fresh new Halloween and Fall Flavored gooides! From some hip new clothing to home decor for a cozy fall festive home or bedroom, we've got plenty to discover for all your take-home wants and needs!

All New Plaza de Los Muertos

This year, we have partnered with the Cultural Del Mexico foundation to put on a stunning Dia de Los Muertos display at Plaza de Mexico in Adventure Park.

From live entertainment to stunning decorations and displays, Plaza de Mexico has been transformed into the Plaza de Los Muertos for a culturally rich celebration like no other!
