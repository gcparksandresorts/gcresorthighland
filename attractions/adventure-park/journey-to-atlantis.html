The Lost City Returns

Thousands of years ago, an advanced aquatic civilization lived in harmony alongside humans. Their coexistence was marked by mutual trade, cultural exchange, and profound friendship, blurring the boundaries between ocean and land. However, the tranquility was shattered by a cataclysmic conflict, which drove the two civilizations apart—humans retreating to the land while the aquatic beings vanished into the depths of the sea, seemingly never to cross paths again.

But the tides of fate churn once more when a routine fishing expedition stumbles upon a breathtaking sight far out at sea: towering castle spires piercing the horizon. Intrigued, the fishermen embark on a journey towards the strange object, only to discover an awe-inspiring sight—a colossal palace floating above the ocean's surface. Welcomed by mysterious aquatic beings, they soon realize that they have stumbled upon the fabled realm of Atlantis, miraculously risen from the depths.

An Aquatic Adventure

Step aboard an Atlantean trade vessel and set sail on a captivating journey into the lost kingdom of Atlantis. Explore the submerged world and encounter the mesmerazing underwater landscapes, exotic marine creatures, and marvel at the treasures of the deep sea. Engage with Atlantean villagers and uncover the history of this curious place, on your very own Joruney to Atlantis!

Know Before You Go

Journey to Atlantis is an indoor water ride with moderate drops, including a thrilling 80-foot plunge. Riders on this attraction may get wet. For your safety, guests with electronic assistive devices are advised to remove them before boarding the attraction.

Attraction Safety: Before visiting, please review our Safety Guidelines and read all posted information at an attraction prior to boarding. Please speak to attraction operators if you have questions or concerns about riding.


All attractions at the Globe-City Resort Highland are subject to closure at anytime. For an updated list of attraction closures, download the GC RESORT mobile app or visit the park directories within Adventure Park.
