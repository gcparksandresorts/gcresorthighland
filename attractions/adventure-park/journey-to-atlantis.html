The Lost City Returns

Thousands of years ago, an advanced aquatic civillization lived in harmony with the humans. The two species traded goods, art, and friendliness and the ocean and land lived as one. Then, after a great war broke out, the two civillizations split ways; the aquatic into the sea and the humans onto land. The two never saw eachother again.

Then one day, a fishing exhpedition saw something unbelieveable far out into the sea: castle spires! Upon arriving to the strange feature, they discovered an enormous palace floating above the ocean waves. The fisherman were welcomed into the palace by some strange aquatic beings and thus discovered that Atlantis had returned!

An Aquatic Adventure

Joyful at the return of unity between Atlantis and the hummans, you are now able to visit the beautiful underwater city.

Know Before You Go

Journey to Atlantis is an indoor water ride featuring small drops and a single large drop 80ft in height. Riders on this attraction may get wet. It is recommended for guests with electronic assistive devices to remove these devices before riding.

Attraction Safety: Before visiting, please review our Safety Guidelines and read all posted information at an attraction prior to boarding. Please speak to attraction operators if you have questions or concerns about riding.


All attractions at the Globe-City Resort Highland are subject to closure at anytime. For an updated list of attraction closures, download the GC RESORT mobile app or visit the park directories within Adventure Park.
