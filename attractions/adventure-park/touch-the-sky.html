Soar to New Heights

Touch the Sky is a high-thrill experience like nothing else. Begin your ride with a nearly 60 mph launch soaring through a 100ft hill and careening through high-banked turns, twists, and soaring jumps. Then, catch your breath on a magentic lift hill before plummeting 80 feet into a unique inversion, second launch, and a 50ft loop!

To top off an awesome roller-coaster experience, Touch the Sky features an energetic arial score composed by the Oras Nou Harmonic Orchestra which compliments the fast-paced ride with an equally thrilling soundtrack.

Know Before You Go

Touch the Sky is an intense launched rollercoaster featuring accelerations of up to 16m/s, high G-Forces, inversions, fast turns, and hard stops. Due to the nature of this ride, it is advised that expectant mothers and guests with recent medical history do not ride. This attraction features over-the-shoulder restraints which may be restricting to some guests.

Attraction Safety: Before visiting, please review our Safety Guidelines and read all posted information at an attraction prior to boarding. Please speak to attraction operators if you have questions or concerns about riding.


All attractions at the Globe-City Resort Highland are subject to closure at anytime. For an updated list of attraction closures, download the GC RESORT mobile app or visit the park directories within Adventure Park.

Single Riders queues may close periodically depending on capacity.
