Soar to New Heights

Touch the Sky will catapult you to unimaginable speeds, propelling you through twists, turns, and loops that will leave you breathless.

Fly into the action with a 60mph launch and then soar through the clouds dropping over 80 feet! All while an adrenaline-pumping areal score, performed by the Oras Nou Harmonic Orchestra, plays on the on-board audio.

Know Before You Go

Touch the Sky is an intense launched rollercoaster with accelerations of up to 16m/s and high levels of G-force. This rollercoaster features multiple inversions, fast launches, hard stops, and high-banked turns. Due to the nature of this ride, it is advised that expectant mothers and guests with recent medical history do not ride. Please be advised that due to the restricting nature of this attraction's restraints, some guests may be unable to ride.

Attraction Safety: Before visiting, please review our Safety Guidelines and read all posted information at an attraction prior to boarding. Please speak to attraction operators if you have questions or concerns about riding.


All attractions at the Globe-City Resort Highland are subject to closure at anytime. For an updated list of attraction closures, download the GC RESORT mobile app or visit the park directories within Adventure Park.

Single Riders queues may close periodically depending on capacity.
