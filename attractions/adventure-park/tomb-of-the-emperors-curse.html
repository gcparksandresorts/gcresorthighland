An Anctient Legend

Emperor Skullallah ruled with an iron fist over the Adventure Region 2,000 years ago. When he died, he ordered that his body be preserved in a granduous tomb full of treasure and antiquities.

However, legend says that after talk circulated around his empire of grave robbers planning to steal his treasure, the emperor forced a spell-caster to place a dark and forboding curse over his tomb. One to forever punish those who dared to take his treasure.

Now, the great Adventures and More company has opened the previously sealed tomb for all those willing to explore! Board an excavation gantry and view the multiple levels of this amazing tomb for yourself. Just don't touch the treasure...

Know Before You Go

Tomb of the Emperor's Curse is an intense indoor elevator ride featuring frequent drops, free fall up to 60ft, intense accelerations, flashing lights, loud noises, and other factors that may be too intense for some guests. It is advised that expectant mothers and those with certain medical conditions do not ride.

Attraction Safety: Before visiting, please review our Safety Guidelines and read all posted information at an attraction prior to boarding. Please speak to attraction operators if you have questions or concerns about riding.


All attractions at the Globe-City Resort Highland are subject to closure at anytime. For an updated list of attraction closures, download the GC RESORT mobile app or visit the park directories within Adventure Park.

Single Riders queues may close periodically depending on capacity.
