Know Before You Go

Tomb of the Emperor's Curse is an intense indoor elevator ride featuring frequent drops, free fall up to 60ft, intense accelerations, flashing lights, loud noises, and other factors that may be too intense for some guests. It is advised that expectant mothers and those with certain medical conditions do not ride.

Attraction Safety: Before visiting, please review our Safety Guidelines and read all posted information at an attraction prior to boarding. Please speak to attraction operators if you have questions or concerns about riding.
