An Anctient Legend

Prepare to be awestruck by the grandeur and mystique of Skullalah's multi-story tomb, intricately crafted with jaw-dropping details that will transport you back thousands of years in history. Dare to venture aboard our gantry lift and unravel the secrets hidden within the tomb's various levels, but heed this warning: the treasure that lies within is cursed!

Legend has it that Emperor Skullalah, a once-powerful ruler with godly powers, foresaw the theft of his priceless treasures from his tomb. To protect his legacy, he cast a malevolent curse upon his burial site, condemning any who dare to lay hands upon his wondrous riches.

As you journey through the depths of Skullalah's tomb, marvel at the ancient artifacts and relics that adorn its chambers, but remember, touching the treasure could unleash the wrath of the emperor's curse upon you!

Are you brave enough to defy the odds and uncover the secrets of the Tomb of the Emperor's Curse?

Adventure awaits... if you dare!

Know Before You Go

Tomb of the Emperor's Curse is an intense elevator ride featuring frequent drops, free falls of up to 70 feet, heart-pounding accelerations, flashing lights, and thunderous noises that may be too intense for some guests. It is advised that expectant mothers and individuals with certain medical conditions, such as heart problems, neck or back issues, motion sickness, or sensitivity to flashing lights and loud sounds, do not ride.

Attraction Safety: Before visiting, please review our Safety Guidelines and read all posted information at an attraction prior to boarding. Please speak to attraction operators if you have questions or concerns about riding.


All attractions at the Globe-City Resort Highland are subject to closure at anytime. For an updated list of attraction closures, download the GC RESORT mobile app or visit the park directories within Adventure Park.

Single Riders queues may close periodically depending on capacity.
