The Magical World of Sweets and Treats

Hope aboard a friendly Magic Cat and take a magical journey into the candied world of Sugarville accompanied by the Magic Bakery's Waffle Witch as she tours you around the farms where she sources her delectible produce.

Find out the flavors that fuel the tasty treats of the Waffle Witch's Magic Bakery and explore a wonderous new world. Watch out, as you may just encounter the mystical wild life while on your journey!

Delectible Dining

After your tour, taste the magic for yourself at the Magic Bakery Store featuring artisan pastries, hand-crafted esspresso beverages, and more!
