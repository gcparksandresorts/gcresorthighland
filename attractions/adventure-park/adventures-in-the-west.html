Old Yander's Adventures in the West

An interactive roleplaying experience. Join the characters of Old Yander's Boom Town on an adventure through the west starting with a newsbreaking occurence somewhere in the town that you and your friends must investigate! Discover all kinds of new stories and characters and find out what lurks beyond the rich gold mines of the boom town.

Attraction Type: Interactive Experience

Attraction Tags: Interactive, Roleplay, Storytelling, Adventure, Unique, Walking

Location: Old Yander's Boom Town, Adventure Park

Attraction Duration: ~ 10 - 40 Minutes


Queuing: No Queuing Required

Accessibility: Guest may remain in wheelchair - Learn More

The Western Gazette

Start your Adventure at the town's source of all happenings and current events: The Western Gazette. Here, the kind journalists will fill you in on the current events and set you off on your Western Adventure!

Investigate and Explore

With the information gathered from the Gazette, explore the town and investigate, aquiring evidence and witness reports from the Town's citizens wherever you can.

Make sure not to leave any stones unturned! Explore nearly a dozen walkthrough areas of the town from the Bank to the Pub and find what characters are lurking inside with possibly some valuable information.
