Merlin's Museum of Curiosity

Take a detour through the forest and visit the wonderous Museum of Curiosity in Hindsborg Village. Here it's unknown what you may encounter!

Attraction Type: Special Effects Show

Attraction Tags: Magical, Musical, Entertainment, Standing

Location: Hindsborg Village, Adventure Park

Attraction Duration: Occurs every half hour , lasts 10 Minutes

Minimum Height: For all ages


Queuing: None

Accessibility: Guest may remain in wheelchair or ECV - Learn More

Viewing Information

Merlin's Museum of Curiosity is always open, so feel free to wander inside. The real magic, however, occurs every half hour (10:00AM, 10:30AM, 11:00AM... etc) when the entire museum comes alive and fills with music and mystique!
Know Before You Go

This attraction features flashing lights and fog effects. Those who may be aggrevated by these effects should not participate.

Attraction Safety: Before visiting, please review our Safety Guidelines and read all posted information at an attraction prior to boarding. Please speak to attraction operators if you have questions or concerns about riding.
