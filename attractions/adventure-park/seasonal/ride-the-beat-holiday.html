Time is Running Out

After his Reindeer got lost in Metropolis, Santa is running out of time to deliver all his presents! If only there was a supersonic way for him to fly through the city and round them all up...

Well now there is! With the brand new Waveriding Vehicles, we can ride the sound-waves of Christmas music through the city and find all of Santa's reindeer with time to spare!

Hop aboard this spirited holiday overlay of a fan-favorite attraction and participate in the adrenaline pumping mission to save Christmas!

Know Before You Go

Ride the Beat: Holiday is an intense rollercoaster in the dark. This ride features accelerations of up to 16m/s, sudden stops, backwards movement, high G-force, and multiple strobe and fog effects. It is advised that expectant mothers, guests with recent medical history, and guests with conditions that may be aggravated by this experience do not ride. Please note, this attraction's restraints may be restricting to some guests.

Attraction Safety: Before visiting, please review our Safety Guidelines and read all posted information at an attraction prior to boarding. Please speak to attraction operators if you have questions or concerns about riding.


All attractions at the Globe-City Resort Highland are subject to closure at anytime. For an updated list of attraction closures, download the GC RESORT mobile app or visit the park directories within Adventure Park.

Single Riders queues may close periodically depending on capacity.
