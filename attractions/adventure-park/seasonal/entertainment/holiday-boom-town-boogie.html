It's the holiday celebration here at Old Yander's Boom Town, and all the villagers are ready to boogie! Join the western cast of characters at Old Yander's Boom Town as they make their way to the Boom Mountain Stage for the annual Boom Town Boogie, hosted by famous Western Folk Band, Struck Gold. Grab your partner, or dance with one of the villagers, and swing around for some western box dancing fun!

The Boom Town Boogie is the talk of the west and it's a coveted holiday event you won't want to miss!

Hosted by Struck Gold, a famous Oras Nou Western Folk Band, you'll be sure to get right into the western holiday spirit featuring covers of some holiday favorites like "Two-Step 'Round the Christmas Tree", "Rodolf the Red Nosed Raindear", "Let it Snow", and more!
