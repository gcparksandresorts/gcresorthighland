Halfway to Halloween

Celebrate October 15th, AKA the Halway point to Halloween!

This is an exclusive entertainment offering for October 15th and is available to all guests visiting Adventure Park on this date.

Attraction Type: Stage Show

Attraction Tags: Spooky, Musical, Comedy, Nighttime

Location: Adventure Plaza Stage, Adventure Park

Attraction Duration: 20 Minutes

Showtimes: See Resort Schedule


Accessibility: Guest may remain in wheelchair or ECV - Learn More

Join the infamous Witch Sisters-- Hocus, Pocus, and Luna -- as they ring in the Halloween season in comedic and musical delight! For on this night, it is the halfway point between a boring Summer Season and the official night of Halloween! The three sisters have been preparing all month long and cannot wait to include you in the festivities!
