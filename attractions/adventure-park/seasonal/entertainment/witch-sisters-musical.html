The Witch Sisters Musical

Join the three Witch Sisters as they prepare for Sally's Halloween Party and encounter many wacky and musical setbacks on their way in this family friendly musical.

Available during Sally's Halloween After-Party only

Attraction Type: Live Entertainment

A Halloween Hoot!

Already running late to Sally's famous halloween party, the three Witch Sisters are scrambling to get ready. That's when one of the sisters suggests that they cast a spell to transform into the most popular party guests. It seems liek a great idea at first but ultimately leads to whacky and chaotic fun. Bring the whole family to this fun musical comedy show and party along with the Whitch Sisters and all their whimsical antics!

Featuring delightful and elaborate musical numbers backed by parodies of popular songs including hits from Abba, Eartha Kit, Rihanna, and more plus some original show tunes, this musical is one to put on your Halloween Party itinerary!
