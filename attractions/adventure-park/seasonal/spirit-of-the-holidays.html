The Spirit of the Holidays Spectacular

Join the wonderful Spirit of the Holidays as he takes you on an adventure to discover the magic of the holiday season. From joy in the air to warm and cozy nights with family, recount the memories and the connections that make the holiday season so magical!

This offering is exclusive to Globe-City's Season of Light Celebration

Attraction Type: Fireworks Spectacular

Attraction Tags: Water, Fireworks, Pyrotechnics, Magic, Entertainment

Location: The Boulevard and Atlantis Castle Plaza, Adventure Park

Attraction Duration: 20 Minutes

Showtimes: See Resort Schedule


Seating: Priority and Standby Standing Areas

Accessibility: MDA accessible seating area available - Learn More

Be Immersed in the Holidays

With a wonderous orchestral overture to kick off the show, The Spirit of the Holidays spectacular whips you right into the magic. Featuring over one-hundred advanced LED water fountains, hundreds of pyrotechnics, a reworked soundtrack, and more, this show is a work of art curated by dozens of creative and talented people.

Music, Magic, and More

Spirit of the Holidays features a unique soundtrack curated by our in-house creative team with the talents of the Oras Nou Harmonic Orchestra and the Minecraftian Jewish Association of Art and Culture. Featuring reworks of some popular holiday music and innovative custom content, the show blends together music from all sorts of cultures and nations to create a unique display of hummanity and how we celebrate the season.

View from Anywhere!

View the dazzling fireworks from anywhere in Adventure Park! But for the full experience, look for viewing on The Boulevard where you can enjoy the show surrounded by advanced Projections on the Boulevard facades, or get a spot in the Atlantis Plaza with excelent views of the water show, the Atlantis Castle projections, and more!

Daring guests can also take spot way up front along the water in our designated splash zone, where you WILL get wet!

Know Before You Go

General viewing for The Spirit of the Holidays Spectacular is restricted to standing viewing only. This includes standing for 15 to 45 minutes. If you or a member of your party cannot stand for this duration, visit Guest Services in the Entrance Plaza to get access to the MDA Accessible viewing area. Benches are not provided in the MDA Viewing Area and wheelchairs or portable chairs will need to be provided.

The Spirit of the Holidays Spectacular may be canceled, delayed, or rescheduled without prior notice due to inclement weather, noise ordinance, operating delays, or other difficulties.

Attraction Safety: Before visiting, please review our Safety Guidelines and read all posted information at an attraction prior to boarding. Please speak to attraction operators if you have questions or concerns about riding.


All attractions at the Globe-City Resort Highland are subject to closure at anytime. For an updated list of attraction closures, download the GC RESORT mobile app or visit the park directories within Adventure Park.
