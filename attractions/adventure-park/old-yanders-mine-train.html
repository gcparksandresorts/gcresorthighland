Hang Onto Yer Hats!

Legend has it that after gold was discovered in the dry land's of the Plains Desert, a man by the name of Old Yander struck up a raggedy mining town to uncover the land's riches. To this day the mine is, by some force of luck, still operating, and now you too can travel deep into its famous rainbow caverns.

Did Y'all See That?!

An old Miner's legend talks about mysterious and mostly unexplainable occurrences within the rickety mine shafts of Yander's boom town. There have been some reports of the minecars derailing and twirling through the endless caverns. Some say its due to the decaying wood structure of the shaft and broken track, others claim its ghosts.

Try it for Yo' Self!

See if the legends ring true as you climb into an actual mine car and set off through the winding rainbow caverns and discover the riches within!
Know Before You Go

Old Yander's Mine Train is an intense rollercoaster featuring spinning elements, large sharp drops, fast turns, and sudden stops and movement. It is recomended that expectant mothers and those with recent medical history do not ride. Please note, the restraints on this attraction may be uncomfortable for larger guests.

Attraction Safety: Before visiting, please review our Safety Guidelines and read all posted information at an attraction prior to boarding. Please speak to attraction operators if you have questions or concerns about riding.
