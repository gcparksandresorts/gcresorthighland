Feel what its like to launch up towards the stars at 40mph on this innovative bungee-style drop ride. Space Jump features fast upwards acceleration and free-fall as you experience the intense forces of space flight.

Exhilerating Views

View Adventure Park from over 100ft in the air and take in the breathtaking scenery around you.
Know Before You Go

Space Jump is an intense drop ride which features frequent and unexpected drops from great heights. This ride may be too intense for some guests and it is advised that expectant mothers and guests with recent medical history do not ride. The restraints of this ride may be restricting to some guests. Prosthetic limbs must be removed before riding.

Attraction Safety: Before visiting, please review our Safety Guidelines and read all posted information at an attraction prior to boarding. Please speak to attraction operators if you have questions or concerns about riding.


All attractions at the Globe-City Resort Highland are subject to closure at anytime. For an updated list of attraction closures, download the GC RESORT mobile app or visit the park directories within Adventure Park.

Single Riders queues may close periodically depending on capacity.
