Board a futuristic Volkswagen Space Car and embark on a tour of the Cosmic Center trading outpost on an elevated roadway.

This attraction is sponsored by Volkswagen

Attraction Type: People Mover

Attraction Tags: Scenic, Slow Moving, Family Friendly, Calm

Location: Cosmic Center, Adventure Park

Minimum Height: None


Queuing: Priority Queuing available

Accessibility: Some vehicles can accomodate wheelchairs - Learn More

Ride in Style

Board into a slick electric Space Car designed specially for Galactic Starway by top-end Volkswagen engineers. Featuring comfortable leather seats, a futuristic sleek design, and onboard music, your ride will be in luxury that's out-of-this-galaxy.

Tour Cosmic Center

As the center point of many galaxies, Cosmic Center is home to many unique species and creatures trading in harmony. Galactic Starway brings you high above Cosmic Center as you traverse an elevated roadway with breathtaking views of the bustling trading outpost and serene music to compliment the visuals.

Tour the CCII

After your elevated ride over the bustling trading center, turn into the Cosmic Center Interspace Institute where you will come face-to-face with astronomical displays of some of the facility's research. You will even travel through their newest Galactic Transporter which will shoot you far into space for outstanding views of the galaxy.
Know Before You Go

Some segments of this attraction feature loud noises, flashing lights, and fog effects. Certain portions of this ride include exposure to the sun for periods longer than 4 minutes. This ride takes place on an elevated roadway, traveling over 15ft above the ground. It is advised that guests having conditions which may be agrevated by this experience do not ride.

Attraction Safety: Before visiting, please review our Safety Guidelines and read all posted information at an attraction prior to boarding. Please speak to attraction operators if you have questions or concerns about riding.
