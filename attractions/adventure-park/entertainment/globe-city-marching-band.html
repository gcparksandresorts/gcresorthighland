The Globe-City Marching Band

Dance along as our very own Marching Band parades down The Boulevard in energized musical fashion! Featuring heart-pounding drums, eclectic trumpets, and a whole musical symphony, it's sure to get you in the mood to groove!

Attraction Type: Live Entertainment

Attraction Tags: Street Show, Standard Entertainment, Musical, Parade

Location: The Boulevard, Adventure Park

Attraction Duration: ~20 Minutes

Showtimes: See Resort Schedule


Accessibility: Guest may remain in wheelchair or ECV - Learn More

The Globe-City Marching band is a musical adventure for the whole family. Dance along as our talented musicians play some familiar tunes and some originals for a fun and exciting performance you won't get anywhere else. Enjoy the show backdroped by the iconic and marvelous Boulevard facades and decorations.
