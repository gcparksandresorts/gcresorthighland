Fly Like a Dragon

On this unique rollercoaster through the mystical forest of Hindsborg, you can feel the thrills of a dragon's magical and wonderous flight. Watch out, as you may even catch a glimpse of Hindsborg's very own purple dragon sleeping in her nest!

With a top speed of 30mph, this family friendly rollercoaster is bound to bring delight and thrills to you and your family through soaring turns and mini dips and lifts.

Sights and Sounds of the Hindsborg Forest

Journey through the magical and mysterious forest of Hindsborg while walking the queue of Flight of the Dragon. Listen carefully as there is magic to be heard all around you! If you peek through the bushes, you just might see a sleeping drat, or even a dragon!
Know Before You Go

Flight of the Dragon is an outdoor rollercoaster featuring mild drops, banked turns, and quick movements. It is recomended that expectant mothers and those with neck or back conditions refrain from experiencing this attraction.

Due to the nature of this attraction, larger guests may be unable to ride.

Attraction Safety: Before visiting, please review our Safety Guidelines and read all posted information at an attraction prior to boarding. Please speak to attraction operators if you have questions or concerns about riding.
