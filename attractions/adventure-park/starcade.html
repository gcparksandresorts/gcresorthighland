Cosmic History

Shortly after the grand-opening of the Cosmic Center Interspace Institute, a great rukus was heard when an alien transport crash landed into the South laboratory. Upon arriving at the wreckage, researchers reported feeling a great sense of excitement and fun-- and 80's music playing on the vehicle's seemingly intact radio. It was later discovered, after some hipster aliens exited the crashed vehicle, that the space ship was actually a trime-tavel vessel which had departed from the 1980s. During some laser testing, the CCII had accidentally shot down their vessel. Since the crash had destroyed much of the laboratories useful equipment, the CCII decided to transorm the space into a wicked 80s themed arcade in apology for shooting down the hipster's space ship.
