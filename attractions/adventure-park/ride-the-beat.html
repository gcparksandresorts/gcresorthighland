Welcome to the Future of Sound

You have been selected to participate in a breakthrough research project to revolutionize the way humans experience sound! You and your party will board into advanced Wave Rider Vehicles, WRVs for short, which are capable of shrinking to a molecular size and riding actual sound waves, like a roller coaster!

Feel the adrenaline surge as your WRV propels you through a symphony of sound, twisting and turning along the exhilarating crescendos and drops. From bass-heavy beats to euphoric melodies, every twist and turn promises an electrifying experience that will leave you breathless.

Your experiment begins in the vibrant depths of Metropolis's bustling Industrial District, home to the city's most exhilarating rave scene. As you enter this underground world of music and dance, get ready to immerse yourself in a sonic wonderland unlike any other.

A State-of-the-Art Attraction

Ride the Beat: Waveriders is an experience like no other. Powered by innovative LABSound Technology, each WRV features 20 speakers and 6 subwoofers delivering a staggering 4000 watts of pure audio power. You'll feel the music pulsating through every fiber of your being, from the deepest bass vibrations to the most intricate melodies, every sound is crystal clear and utterly captivating.

So buckle up and prepare to be blown away on an unforgettable journey through the very fabric of sound itself!

Know Before You Go

Ride the Beat: Waveriders is an intense rollercoaster in the dark. This ride features accelerations of up to 16 meters per second, sudden stops, backwards movement, and high G-forces. Portions of this ride also include fog and strobe lighting effects. Due to the nature of this experience, we advise that expectant mothers, guests with recent medical history, and those with conditions that may be aggravated by intense physical experiences refrain from riding. Please be aware that the restraints used on this attraction may be restrictive for some guests.

Attraction Safety: Before visiting, please review our Safety Guidelines and read all posted information at an attraction prior to boarding. Please speak to attraction operators if you have questions or concerns about riding.
