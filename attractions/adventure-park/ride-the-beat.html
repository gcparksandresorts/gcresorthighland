Welcome to the Future of Sound

You have been selected to participate in a breakthrough research project to revolutionize the way humans experience sound! You and your party will board into advanced Wave Rider Vehicles, or WRVs for short, which are capable of shrinking to a molecular size and riding actual sound waves, like a roller coaster!

To test the new WRVs, your experiment will take place in Metropolis's hottest rave known for its heart pounding music and wild parties. The rave is located deep in the Metropolis Industrial District, which just so happens to be the perfect discrete location to conduct the WRV testing.

State-of-the-Art Audio Experience

Each WRV is powered by LAB Sound technology with one of the most advanced coaster-audio systems in the world. With over 20 speakers and 6 subwoofers totalling 4000 watts per vehicle, your WRV will launch you right into the action for a rockin'-ride like no other!
Know Before You Go

Ride the Beat: Waveriders is an intense rollercoaster in the dark. This ride features accelerations of up to 16m/s, sudden stops, backwards movement, high G-force, and multiple strobe and fog effects. It is advised that expectant mothers, guests with recent medical history, and guests with conditions that may be aggravated by this experience do not ride. Please note, this attraction's restraints may be restricting to some guests.

Attraction Safety: Before visiting, please review our Safety Guidelines and read all posted information at an attraction prior to boarding. Please speak to attraction operators if you have questions or concerns about riding.
