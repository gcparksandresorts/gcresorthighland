Conquer the Crusade

Take a ride on the roughest ride in the wilderness as you explore the forboding jungle around Adventure Plains. The jungle is home to many creatures and forgotten civilizations, it's unknown what you might discover!

But beware! Legend has it a curse lies under the surface of these iteresting landscapes, one protecting the rich jungle treasure we have yet to discover...

Know Before You Go

Crusader is an intense steel rollercoaster featuring large drops, high-banked turns, large g-forces, airttime, and other intense elements. It is advised that expectant mothers and those with recent medical history do not ride. Portions of this ride also feature strobe effects, fog, and loud noises.

Attraction Safety: Before visiting, please review our Safety Guidelines and read all posted information at an attraction prior to boarding. Please speak to attraction operators if you have questions or concerns about riding.


All attractions at the Globe-City Resort Highland are subject to closure at anytime. For an updated list of attraction closures, download the GC RESORT mobile app or visit the park directories within Adventure Park.

Single Riders queues may close periodically depending on capacity.
