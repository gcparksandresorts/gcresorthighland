Conquer the Crusade

Conquer the Crusade on our tallest and longest roller coaster. Featuring a massive 120ft drop into the dense wilderness and two cable-lift hills, Crusader is an intense experience recomended only for the most brave Adventurers.

Begin your journey with a slow moving pass-through of recently uncovered Mayan ruins in near-perfect condition. Rumor has it that some explorers can still here the ritual chants of the anctient tribes from long ago. Then, gain altitude as you are lifted above the lush vegitation and then air dropped deep into the jungle. Zip and glide on a smooth steel track as you careen through the wilderness at over 40mph.

Crusader is an immersive roller coaster experience and features a custom soundtrack which interplays with the ride for an action-packed unforgettable adventure!

Know Before You Go

Crusader is an intense steel rollercoaster featuring large drops, high-banked turns, large g-forces, airttime, and other intense elements. It is advised that expectant mothers and those with recent medical history do not ride. Portions of this ride also feature strobe effects, fog, and loud noises.

Due to the nature of this attraction, larger guests may be unable to ride.

Attraction Safety: Before visiting, please review our Safety Guidelines and read all posted information at an attraction prior to boarding. Please speak to attraction operators if you have questions or concerns about riding.
