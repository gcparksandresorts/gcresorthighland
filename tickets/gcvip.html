Perks and Benefits

Priority Queue Access

Priority Queues can greatly shorten your attraction waittimes with exclusive access to GC VIP ticketholders. Restrictions apply including limited uses per-attraction, 'cool-down' time periods between uses, and time-of-day restrictions. For more information on restrictions and limitations of priority queue access, view our GC VIP Terms and Conditions

Entertainment Priority Seating

For our live entertainment and nighttime spectaculars, GC-VIP guests with Gold and Deluxe tickets can enter our priority seating areas for a better view of the show without the hassle of reserving a seat. Priority seating areas open 1-hour before showtime. Priority Seating areas are subject to capacity limitations.

VIP Lounges

Our exclusive VIP lounges feature comfortable indoor seating away from theme park crowds. Vending machines, water refill stations, and complimentary WIFI are also available. GC-VIP guests with Gold and Deluxe tickets can access our VIP lounges by scanning their tickets at the entrance. Lounge space is first-come first-serve and some lounges may be closed or unavailable during your visit. To find VIP lounges, use a park guide map or the GC RESORT mobile app.

Preffered Parking

GC VIP Admission does not include parking, however, guests visiting with pre-purchased GC VIP Admission can show their tickets at the parking booth for a 10% Discount on Preffered Parking in the Adventure Parking Lot only.

Complimentary GC Photo Pass

GC VIP Gold and Deluxe admission includes GC PhotoPass free-of-charge. To activate, download the GC RESORT mobile app and set up GC PhotoPass using your GC VIP ticket ID.
