Frequently Asked Questions

How Can I Check my Blackout Dates?

Passport Black-Out dates are determiend by each date's Admission Tier There are a few ways to check the Admission Tier for a select date:
  • Visit the Resort Information page to view the current Admission Tier
  • View the Resort Operating Calendar to view Admission Tiers for the next 5 months
  • Call the Annual Passholder Office at (312) 988 1300to inquire about black-out dates and other passholder matters

Can I still visit the park on a black-out date?

Yes! Annual Passholders can purchase discounted admission through the Annual Passholder portal for themselves as well as other guests on any date including black-out dates

What if I lose my passport card?

Lost or stolen passport cards can be re-printed at any ticket booth in the Esplenade Plaza or the Will Call booth.

Fees for Re-Printing a Passport: $5

Once a passport is reprinted, the original passport card will become invalid and unusable

How / when do I renew my passport?

Passports are valid for 12-months following the first date of use. Once the 12-month period has elapsed, guests have 14 days to renew their passport through the Passholder Portal for a discounted price.

If the passport is not renewed within the 14 day period, guests will need to purchase a new annual passport through the Ticketing Portal

All passport add-ons and upgrades expire after 12-months following their purchase. If a passport is not renewed after 14 days of its expiration, all add-ons and upgrades will be removed and cannot be transfered to a new passport.

During the 14-day post-expiration period, the Annual Passport will not grant access to Adventure Park unless renewed.
