How do I link photos?

Ride Photos

To link ride photos, look for the screens near the attraction exit with your photo displayed. Under your photo will be a 6 digit code. On the GC RESORT mobile app, locate the PhotoPass button -> click "Link Photos" -> enter the 6-digit code into the text box -> click "Link"

Your photo will then be available within 15-20 minutes on the GC RESORT app.

Photo Spot Photos

After taking a picture with one of our in-park photographers, they will provide you a barcode to scan inorder to link your photos.

On the GC RESORT app, click "PhotoPass" -> "Link Photos" -> "Scan Barcode" -> scan the photographer's barcode.

Your photos will appear within 15-20 minutes.

More instructions are detailed on the GC RESORT mobile app.

How do I view my photos?

To view your linked photos, go to "PhotoPass" on the GC RESORT app, then press "View Photos".
To download your photos, click "Download" and select the photos you would like to download.
