Highland's Happiest Halloween Celebration

Sally's Spooktacular Halloween After-Party is an annual nighttime event for the whole family located at Adventure Park. This event runs annually from September to the end of October and will begin at 7PM on select dates. Sally's Halloween After-Party is a separate ticket event meaning Adventure Park will be closed off to day guests during event hours. Expect fewer crowds and shorter attraction wait times! Sally's Halloween Party also features tons of exclusive entertainment and offerings for a night of tricks, treats, and spookctacular fun!

Entertainment and Attractions

During event hours, various themed entertainment opportunities will be setup throughout Adventure Park including a villainous dance party, a caroling ghost choir and an event specific nighttime fireworks spooktacular. In addition to live entertainment, there's lots more to do including trick-or-treating throughout Adventure Park, a ride aboard Mission: Overtaken, exclusive Halloween character greetings, and family-friendly arts & crafts stations throughout the park.

Exclusive Food and Drink Offerings

This Halloween, Adventure Park has more treats than tricks! Visit some of your favorite restaurants in Adventure Park for some spooky sweets and savory Halloween offerings or try something new at event exclusive food and drink carts. For the adults, event exclusive alcoholic beverages will be available throughout Adventure Park and for the family, explore our pop-up treat stops for some Halloween fun for your tastebuds!

Merchandise and More

Before the party begins, get ready to celebrate with some event exclusive accessories including light-up necklaces, head bands, and more! Then, take the party home with Sally's Halloween After-Party exclusive merchandise including frighteningly fashionable apparel, keepsakes, and more! Available during event hours only at any of our in-park gift-shops.

Limited Availability of Tickets - Tickets for Sally's Halloween After-Party are sold on a first-come first-serve basis and may sell out for certain nights
