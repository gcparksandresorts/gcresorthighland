Highland's Most Magical Celebration

Globe-City's Season of light Celebration is an annual event that takes over the entire resort. This event is included with general admission and features exclusive attractions including in-park decorations, new shows and entertainment, and exclusive rides and experiences.

When is Season of Light?
This event occurs annually from the first Friday of November until the end of the first week of January. Although decorations will begin taking shape November 1st, all entertainment and exclusive event offerings will not be available until the event officially begins.

Important Guidelines - Always review our Safety Guidelines before visitng the resort

Spirit of the Holidays and other outdoor entertainment is subject to cancelation or delay on a nightly basis due to weather or other concerns.
Season of Light merchandise and food items are limited in quantity and sold while supplies last.
