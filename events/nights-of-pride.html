Giving Back to the Community We Celebrate

In spirit of Pride and all things Love and Harmony, Globe-City Parks and Resorts will donate 50% of the cost-reduced profits from Adventurous Nights of Pride ticket sales to local LGBTQ+ charities including The Trevor Project, Community Minecraftia, Loving Hearts Oras Nou, and more!

Important Considerations

Due to the nature of this event, Oras Nou Legislation prevents us from allowing any children under the age of 3 to participate. Learn More

Article P923-S9 of the Oras Nou Public Recreational Activites Legislation states that events involving glamorized sale of alcohol, loud music, abrormal/ festival lighting and decor, adult themes, high volume of attendees, and other potentially high-risk features must be locked to certain age groups.

Due to the nature of this event, it has been decided that Globe-City Parks and Resorts must deny entry to guests under the age of 3 years old in order to comply with this legislation.

If you would like to learn more about Article P923-S9 and the reasoning behind this decision, visit www.on.gov/legislation/documents/ONPRAL/p923-s9
