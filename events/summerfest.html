Everything You Need to Know

Globe-City's Summer Fest Celebration is a seasonal event which takes place between Late May through August. Admission to this event is included with general admission to Globe-City's Adventure Park. Dive into the vibrant spirit of summer as Adventure Park transforms into a haven of excitement and joy. From thrilling new activities to captivating entertainment offerings, delectable food and drink options, and so much more, there's something for everyone to enjoy!

Embrace the warmth of the season with your family and friends as you create cherished memories that will last a lifetime. Every corner of Adventure Park is brimming with the magic of summer, waiting to be explored and enjoyed by all.
