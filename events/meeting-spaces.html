Important Considerations

Events hosted at the Globe-City Resort Highland are required to follow strict safety and operation guidelines. Guests on Globe-City Resort property are required to adhere to the resort's Safety Guidelines and are subject to removal from the event and resort property should these rules be violated.

Events hosting more than 30 attendees must provide security. For your convenience, security services are available through Globe-City Resorts Security Services.

In order to reserve a meeting space, you or your company must provide the minimum-payment and security deposit before your rental is reserved. Should you cancel or reschedule your event, a cancelation fee may apply.
