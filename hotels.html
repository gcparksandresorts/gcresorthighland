Adventure Hour

An Extra Hour of Play

With Adventure Hour, guests staying in the Lakeside Hotel or the Aztech Resort can gain an extra hour of play in Adventure Park before the park opens to regular guests.
During this hour, limited attractions will be open, however, guests may enjoy breakfast at the Boulevard Diner or Exhpadition Coffee House and can catch a ride on the Ghostbuster's Experience, Ride the Beat: Waveriders, and the Galactic Starway
  • Adventure Hour is only available on select dates
  • The additional fee for Adventure Hour changes depending on the date selected
  • Guests must be staying in a Resort Hotel to purchase Adventure Hour
  • Valid park admission for date selected required to purchase Adventure Hour
